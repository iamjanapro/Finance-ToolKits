# Nanogotchi Auditor: Production Edition

## About Nanogotchi
Nanogotchi is a blockchain-based "play-to-earn" ecosystem where users cultivate virtual plants to earn Nano.

**Asset Accumulation:** Growth is driven by strategic claiming and selling of game assets like "broccoli".

**Yield Mechanics:** Different plants (Starter, Fast, Big, Golden, Tree) offer varying hourly yields and growth stages.

**Micro-Economy:** The game allows players to convert time and game strategy into liquid cryptocurrency assets.

## About Our Product
The Nanogotchi Auditor is a high-precision financial tracking tool built to manage the complexities of this virtual economy. It serves as a personal "ledger and projector" for serious asset accumulators.

### Key Features:

**Real-Time Portfolio Valuation:** Automatically calculates the total Nano value of your wallet broccoli based on current market sell prices.

**Smart Ledger Integration:** Tracks every transaction type, including claims, deposits, withdrawals, and "faucet" income, to maintain an accurate cost basis.

####Advanced Production Analytics:

**Yield Tracker:** Real-time calculation of your hourly broccoli yield.

**Daily Revenue:** Live projections of daily Nano earnings based on current plant inventory.

**Visual Data Insights:** Includes production trends and price history charts for better decision-making.

**Future Projections:** Provides a breakdown of expected broccoli and Nano accumulation over 1, 7, 30, 100, and 365-day periods.

## User Guide: Operating the Auditor

1. Initial Setup
**Set Market Price:** Enter the current Nano sell price in the "Set Market Price" card and click Set. This anchors all your ROI and Portfolio calculations to real-time market data.

**Inventory Sync:** Enter your current number of plants in the "Plant Inventory" section. The auditor supports Starter, Fast, Big, Golden, and Tree varieties.

2. **Recording Activity**
**Claiming Broccoli:** When you harvest from the game, select Broccoli Claimed from the transaction dropdown. Enter the amount to automatically update your wallet balance and ledger.

**Feeding Plants:** Select Feed Plant to deduct broccoli from your wallet. This ensures your "Total Portfolio Value" remains accurate after reinvesting assets into growth.

**Liquidating Assets:** When you sell broccoli for Nano, use the Broccoli Sell option. The auditor will calculate the Nano received and add it to your Nano Balance field.

3. **Data Management (JSON Export/Import)**
Because this tool uses a "Zero-Footprint" approach, your data is stored locally in your browser. To move your data between your laptop and mobile:

**Export JSON:** Click the Export JSON button to download a file containing your entire transaction history and plant inventory.

**Import:** On a new device, click Import, select your backup file, and the auditor will perfectly reconstruct your entire financial history.

**Snapshot:** Use the 📸 Snapshot button to generate a clean, shareable image of your current stats and charts for your daily Logseq reports.
